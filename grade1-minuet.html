Minuet in C


W.A Mozart


This is a piece Mozart wrote when he was about 7 years old.


It is from a collection of songs put together to teach his elder sister named Nannerl to play piano.


You can use the video to listen and follow along with the sheet music and to learn the correct fingerings.





How to play this song


Be seven!


Remember that Mozart was only seven years old when he wrote this song. Be giddy, playful and cheeky to get in the right frame of mind to play this piece.


Hand Independence

Split your hands up. Practice your right hand first, then your left. When you are ready put both of them together.




Fingerings


Pay attention to your ABRSM score. The correct fingerings are printed on top of the bar. Use these fingerings for your exam.


Legato

Pay attention to the legato marks on your score in bars 1, 3, 9, 11. You must practice pressing one hand down while lifting the other back up.
