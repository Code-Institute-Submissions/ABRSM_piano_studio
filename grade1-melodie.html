Melodie


Robert Schumann


This is a piece Robert Schumann wrote in 1848 for his 3 daughters.


The album is a collection of 43 short pieces. The first part (pieces 1-18) are pieces that are suitable for children or beginners to play.


You can use the video to listen and follow along with the sheet music and to learn the correct fingerings.





How to play this song


Theme


Your score will have "p" written, but the actual feel should be more like a crescendo and diminuendo.




Dynamics


Draw your attention to bars 5-7. Picture the sea with waves crashing down on each other. Each bar should be a little louder than the last to create a "wall of waves" type of effect here.



Special fingerings


Pay attention to bars 8 and 11. Notice the fingerings 4-5 in bar 8 and 3-5 in bar 11. This is to indicate that you must switch fingers quickly before you play the next note.
