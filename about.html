About


Hello, I'm CC


I teach piano!


I'm from China, and I've been playing piano since I was 7 years old. I graduated from Beijing Normal University in 2013 and have been teaching piano ever since.


In China I liked teaching kids, helping them to prepare for piano recitals and the Chinese piano exams. Now, I live in Ireland and am focusing on ABRSM exams.


Unfortunately, due to covid, it is not possible to teach face to face at the moment, so I have decided to start a new website and try to reach students online.


The content is 100% free, and if you have not done so already you can subscribe here to be updated on any new releases.


You can contact me through the social media links below and I hope you enjoy your time at my piano studio.
